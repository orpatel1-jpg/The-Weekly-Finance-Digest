About This Website

Our Purpose

The Weekly Finance Digest was created to make financial news easier to understand for students and young adults. Many finance topics can feel confusing, so this website breaks them down into simple and clear explanations.

Who It’s For

This website is designed for college students and young adults who want to learn about money, markets, and financial decisions without feeling overwhelmed.
